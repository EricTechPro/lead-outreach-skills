Lead Outreach — email template gallery

Five styles, tiered by deliverability. For cold 1:1 outreach, pick a ✅ template (plain or light) — research shows plain text gets ~23% higher opens and dodges spam filters. ⚠️ templates are for warm lists / newsletters only. Sample content is the same in each so you can compare the look, not the words.
